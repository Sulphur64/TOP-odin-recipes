AQVT, the Eternal spirit

First, you must find meat. Any meat. Deer, boar even rabbit. Refrain from using the flesh of your enemy if you have a choice.

he will speak ill of you in the other world, even if you don't hear this voice.

The ingredients

  • meat
  • vinegar
  • berries

Steps

  1. slice the meat. the thinner it is, the fastest it will be ready
  2. soak it in the vinegar, add sweet berries and everything that can enhance the taste of the meat
  3. let it rest fort a day
  4. hang it, outside of animals reach. The place must be not too warm, not too cold
  5. after three days observe it, you will know how you like it
