AQVT, the Eternal spirit

Do not abuse of this liquid. Never drink in an unsafe place. Remember the older it gets, the stronger it gets too.

Only impurities can weaken it. A lot like you.

The ingredients

  • A still
  • Two thick clay pots
  • Empty bottles
  • Cups and a louch
  • A basket of your summer fruits
  • Honey if your berries are not sweet enough
  • Yeast

Preparation of the wine

  1. Choose your fruits well
  2. Wash them before slicing them, remove the seeds
  3. Crush them in a pot, pour honey if necessary.The sweetest it is, the fastest it turns to spirit.
  4. Take your other pot and lay honey then fruit pur√©e in alternance, do not fill to the brim, leave three fingers.
  5. Dissolve your thumb worth of dry yeast in a cup of warm water. Pour it evenly in the pot. Add six cups of water.
  6. Put a plate underneath and on top of it. Let it ferment in a dry, tempered place. It will bubble, maybe overflow. Good.
  7. After a week, the yeast must do its glorious work effectively. stir gently the pot once a week for a month.
  8. At the end of this month, Bottle it then seal it. This wine may be young but will age well.

Preparation of the spirit

  1. Take your still, clean it. Always clean it first.
  2. Take a pot, pour water in it. the still should not touch fire, the warmth of the water is enough
  3. Pour your wine in the still, leave a quarter empty.If you never used a still, consult Ingvar the Permanent
  4. Keep the condenser cold, and wait for the alcohol to come out.use small cups, be attentive to the odor and the color.
  5. The drip must be slow but steady, do not be hasty.The spirit will come in four forms.
    1. The head: this part is not made for humans. Fire spirits will happily drink it.
    2. The first: a few time after the head, keep it for another distillation
    3. The heart: you will smell the fruits, without the solvent, the liquid will be clear
    4. The tail: the smell will be nearly gone, the liquid muddy. You can discard it.
  6. Clean your still, appreciate your hard work.
  7. You should not smell nor taste the solvents or bad spirits. Aerate it if you do. They are not meant to stay in it.
  8. Once the pungent smell and taste is gone, bottle it. This elixir will keep your warm and cool headed if you do not abuse it.
